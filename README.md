# -Urban-Mobility-Analysis-Dashboard
The Urban Mobility Analysis Dashboard provides an interactive visualization of how weather conditions, festivals, road types, and time factors influence traffic congestion and travel times in urban environments.
The goal of this dashboard is to help urban planners, transport authorities, and data analysts understand congestion trends, optimize traffic flow, and improve city mobility strategies.

##Dashboard Highlights
 1. Trip Count by Weather Condition:Displays trip proportions under different weather types such as Clear, Cloudy, Rain, Fog, and Storm.
 2. Average Travel Time by Road Type:Compares average travel times on congested, freeway, and urban roads.
 3. Congestion by Sourceâ€“Destination:Shows congestion indices between key city areas (e.g., Dadar, Andheri, Juhu, Ghatkopar).
 4. Congestion Trend Over Date:Visualizes how congestion levels fluctuate across major festivals like Diwali, Eid, Ganesh Chaturthi, and Christmas/New Year.
 5. Impact of Festivals and Weather on Congestion:Analyzes how seasonal events and weather jointly affect traffic congestion.
 6. Average Travel Time by Day of Week:Highlights weekday vs. weekend congestion patterns to support route and time optimization.
